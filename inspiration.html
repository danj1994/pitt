This designer toolkit has taken inspiration from many things, the main piece of inspiration I have taken from is a game a use to play called fallout 3, the Pipboy is the inspired piece taken from that game and turned into my own design that I believe reflects Retro Futurism. Other inspirations I have drawn upon are the ideas of the Retro Futurism, a time throughout the 60s where designers and artists alike created the idea of the future and how it would look and what technology would be like in the 20th century.

The Jetsons is a cartoon created in the movement of Retro Futurism when the average person drives a flying automobile and lives in a space ship themes home, where the everyday life is now lived in the skies. I love the styles of Retro futurism, when designers took aesthetics of their year and implemented them into flying cars, trains and outer space living.
